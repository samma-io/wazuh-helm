NOTE: The OpenSearch internal_users.yml contains pre-computed bcrypt hashes for
the default passwords. If you change credentials.indexer.password or
credentials.dashboard.password, you must also update the hashes by running
securityadmin.sh inside the wazuh-indexer pod.
